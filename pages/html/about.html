TC Environmental Services Painting

Experienced Home Painters For Over 3 Generations and Counting!

My Name is Dimitrios Kirgiorgis, I started TC Environmental Services Painting in 2003 and have since completed thousands of projects of all sizes across LA County and Orange County. I got my start when I was 7 years old and I'd go painting with my father over summer breaks. By the time I was in high school I could tackle whole homes and business complexes on my own.

I chose to follow in the family tradition like my father and grandfather and I couldn't be happier. I'm lucky I found my passion at 7 years old and I'm happy I get to share it with others. So when you choose TC Environmental Services Painting to paint your home or business, you get the combined knowledge of 60 years passed on from each generation. Painting is what we do.
