San Diego Tree Safety

If you have a tree that is in front of your house causing potential safety hazards, contact TC Environmental Services to assess the situation. You may need to hire our team to take immediate action, or possibly to provide a year-round plan for safety during high winds or rain. Take a look at the trees and your roof. If it looks like a tree could potentially fall and cause injuries, you may wish to have it trimmed back now.

Weak trees can become risks. Risk assessment takes into account the safety of the people and the condition of the tree. Warning signs may include a leaning tree, a buckling tree, or soil that is heaving up around the base of the tree. Look for big cavities, seams or cracks. Defective trees may or may not need to be removed immediately. Some tree problems can be addressed, and some diseases can be removed sparing the life of the tree.
